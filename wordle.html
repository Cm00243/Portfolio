Guess the word:

Game Rules

The rules are the same as Wordle. The word you are trying to guess is 5 letters. No two letters will be used twice in the word. An example of an impossible word is fleet. There are two e's, so it is not a possible word.

A red square means the letter is not used, a yellow square means it is used but in a different place, and a green square shows that you have the correct letter and placement.
